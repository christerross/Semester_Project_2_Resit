Welcome to the game that started it all, Pong!
In this arcade game you will battle against the most difficult computer in the world. Can you do the impossible and beat it?

The goal of the game is to score points by getting the ball to hit the wall behind your opponents paddle. Use your mouse to control your paddle and hit the ball.
